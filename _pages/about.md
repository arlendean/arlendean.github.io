Hello! I am an assistant professor of Supply Chain, Operations, and Technology at the [Olin Business School][1] at [Washington University in St. Louis][2]. Prior to joining Olin, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the [University of Oxford][3]'s [Saïd Business School][4]. I earned my Ph.D. in [Industrial and Operations Engineering][5] at the [University of Michigan][6].

My research centers on data-driven decision-making in complex and uncertain environments. Drawing on optimization and machine learning, I design algorithms that guide real-time operational decisions and study how AI can best support the people who use it. Much of my work is motivated by applications in [healthcare][7] and [revenue management][7].
